Pesto pasta

Click here to get a recipe (for 4 people)
Ingredient Amount
Pasta 400g
Pesto 8 tbsp
Parmesan 100g
  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain the water and keep one cup of drained water on the side to use later.
  2. Add pesto to the pasta and add drained water if the pasta mixture is dry.
  3. Put pesto pasta on the plate and toss with parmesan cheese, also add fresh pepper and olive oil.
